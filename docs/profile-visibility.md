# 🔓 Membership Profile Visibility

Learn how to make your **Crystal-Studio-Community** membership public on your GitHub profile page! 🌟

---

## 👥 Why make it public?
By default, when you accept an invitation to join a GitHub organization, your membership is set to **Private**. Making it public displays the organization's badge on your personal GitHub profile, showcasing your participation in the community!

---

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step Guide

### 1️⃣ Step 1: Open the People list
Go to the **[Crystal-Studio-Community Homepage](https://github.com/Crystal-Studio-Community)** and click on the **People** tab in the navigation menu.

*(Drop your step 1 screenshot at `static/assets/github1.jpg`)*

### 2️⃣ Step 2: Search for your username
Type your GitHub username in the search box to locate your name in the directory.

*(Drop your step 2 screenshot at `static/assets/github2.jpg`)*

### 3️⃣ Step 3: Switch Private to Public
Click the dropdown on the right side of your username row and change the setting from **Private** to **Public**.

*(Drop your step 3 screenshot at `static/assets/github3.jpg`)*

---

🎉 **You are all set!** Your profile badge is now visible to everyone. Check it out on your GitHub profile page!
